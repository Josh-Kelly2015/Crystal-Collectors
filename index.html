Welcome to CrystalsCollector!

Match your Total Score seen in the long blue rectangle with the Goal in the green box!

You can do this by clicking the Crystals located at the center of the page!

Each Crystal has its own hidden Value! You will have to click each one to determine each Crystals worth!

When you match your score in the Blue Rectangle with the Goal number in the Green Box then you win! After you win the game is reset and you are awarded one point to your Win Stat.

If you go over the Goal then you lose!! After you lose the game is reset and you are given one point to your Losses Stat.

*Remember! Each time you win, or lose a game, each Crystal is given a brand new value! Click each one to figure out what they are. *
